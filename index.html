THE WHIO 🦆

Cool facts

  • Whio only show up where the ecosystem is balanced and the rivers are healthy and clean so they can be used as a sort of tracker to see how the environment is doing

  • Their bill is interesting - The upper bill has a soft black flesh like flap that provides protection (a sort of shock absorber) and helps them forage. The lower bill has comb-like structures to help sieve out food from the water. The bill itself will also flush with blood turning the bill a distinct pink colour when angry, threatened or frightened.

  • Whio are monogamous and partner for life unless an incident occurs that causes death or displacement. However, there are some rare cases where one may choose to leave the relationship.
